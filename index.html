Crafting exceptional Romanian heritage wines in the heart of Dealu Mare region.

Explore Our Blends

Sun, Soil & Tradition

Located on the same geographical parallel as Bordeaux and Tuscany, the Urlați vineyards enjoy the perfect balance of intense sunshine and rich clay-limestone soil.

We blend ancient traditions with modern winemaking technology to create unique varieties that tell the story of our hills.

The Signature Selection

A taste of true Dealu Mare terroir.

Fetească Neagră

The king of Romanian reds. Deep ruby color with intense notes of black currants, prunes, and a hint of vanilla spice.

Pelinu de Urlați

A traditional local treasure. A complex, aromatic wine subtly infused with wormwood and alpine herbs.

Fetească Regală

A crisp, elegant white wine presenting vibrant notes of green apple, white flowers, and citrus fruit.
